Passport Application Information

Personal Information

  1. Full Name: As per your official records.
  2. Date of Birth: As per your birth certificate or other official documents.
  3. Place of Birth: City, state, and country.
  4. Gender
  5. Marital Status
  6. Educational Qualification
  7. Employment Details: If applicable.
  8. Address: Present and permanent address with proof.

Documents Required

  1. Proof of Date of Birth:
    1. Birth Certificate
    2. School leaving certificate
    3. Matriculation Certificate
    4. PAN card (if issued before a certain date)
    5. Aadhaar card/E-Aadhaar
  2. Proof of Address (any one of the following):
    1. Aadhaar card/E-Aadhaar
    2. Voter ID card
    3. Electricity bill
    4. Telephone bill
    5. Bank account statement
    6. Rent agreement
  3. Identity Proof (any one of the following):
    1. Aadhaar card/E-Aadhaar
    2. Voter ID card
    3. Driving license
    4. PAN card
  4. Passport-Sized Photographs: As per the specifications provided by the passport office.

Additional Documents for Specific Cases

  1. For Minors:
    1. Birth certificate
    2. Parents' passport copies
  2. For Government Employees:
    1. No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the employer

Online Application Process

  1. Register on the Passport Seva website: Passport Seva
  2. Fill the Application Form: Complete the form with all required details.
  3. Schedule an Appointment: Select a Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) and book an appointment.
  4. Pay the Fees: Pay the applicable fees online.
  5. Visit the PSK/Regional Passport Office (RPO): Carry the original documents and a printout of the application receipt.

Offline Application Process

  1. Collect the Application Form: Obtain it from the nearest Passport Office or download it from the Passport Seva website.
  2. Fill the Application Form: Manually fill in all the required details.
  3. Attach the Required Documents: Attach photocopies of all necessary documents.
  4. Submit the Application Form: Submit it at the nearest Passport Office or Passport Seva Kendra.
  5. Pay the Fees: Pay the applicable fees at the submission center.
  6. Verification: Attend the appointment for document verification and biometric data collection.
